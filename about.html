My Story

I am a college student at Harding University. I am studying Computer Science and breadmaking is a big hobby of mine. While some have mentioned to me if I love it so much I should make a career of it, I believe it would be best to leave it as a hobby. I am uncertain that I would love it as much if I had to wake up at 4 am every morning to do it. I also have a passion for Computer Science and most of all the important people in my life and the friends I have here at Harding.

My passion for breadmaking was something given to me by my Aunt and Grandma. The first person to teach me something about breadmaking was my Aunt when she taught me to make dinner rolls. Later my grandma taught me to make buttermilk biscuits which are still one of my favorite things to make to this day. For a few years after this though I stopped making bread and lost interest and the book my aunt had gotten me collected dust. Thankfully I finally gave it a shot again and have been working hard teaching myself all there is to know for about a year at this point.
